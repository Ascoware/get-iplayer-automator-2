## What is it?

Get iPlayer Automator 2 is a macOS SwiftUI application for downloading BBC iPlayer and STV content. It wraps the Perl-based `get_iplayer` command-line tool and `yt-dlp` for video downloads.

Series-Link/PVR functionality ensures you never miss your favourite shows. Programmes are fully tagged and added to TV.app or Music.app automatically upon completion.

[Download the latest release.](https://github.com/Ascoware/get-iplayer-automator-2/releases)
